About Khorn Chitra

My Story

Hello! I'm Khorn Chitra, a passionate and curious student from Cambodia. My journey at NUCK so far has been defined by three core passions: technology, the English language, and gaming.

This blog is a personal project where I share my thoughts, document my learning process, and connect with people who share similar interests. Whether it's the latest in AI, a new English idiom I learned, or my favorite video game, you'll find it here.

I believe in lifelong learning and using technology to make a positive impact. I'm excited to see what the future holds and to share my adventures with you.

My Journey at National University of Cheasim Kamchaymear

Year 1: The Beginning

Started my university journey at NUCK, discovering my passion for Computer Science. Focused on foundational courses and joined a study group to improve my English speaking skills.

Year 2: Finding My Voice

Launched this personal blog to document my learning and connect with others. Started exploring web development and AI, and began dedicating more time to my gaming hobby.

Future Goals

My goals include completing my degree, securing a tech internship, and continuing to build my skills. I also aspire to travel and experience new cultures, all while sharing my journey here.
